Karthik Vaidhyanathan

Ph.D. Student

Karthik Vaidhyanathan is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Gran Sasso Science Institute, Italy. He is from Sekharipuram, a village in the city of Palakkad, Kerala, India. Prior to joining GSSI, he was working as a Product Lead / Data Scientist in Knowledge Lens Pvt. Ltd, Bengaluru, India. He holds two master degrees, M.Tech in Computer Science specialized in Machine Learning and Data Science from Amrita University, India and MSc in Computer Science specialized in Software Architecture from University of L’Aquila, Italy.

His major research interests include Software Architecture and Machine Learning. He is currently working under the supervision of Dr. Henry Muccini, Associate Professor, University of L’Aquila, Italy for his Doctoral Thesis. More information can be found on his LinkedIn page.
